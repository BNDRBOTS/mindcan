Frequently asked questions

Is the app really free?

Yes, the Starter plan gives you unlimited cards, connections, local saving, and all export formats except PDF. No account needed.

How does AI synthesis work?

You need to provide your own DeepSeek API key in the code. The app sends your card data and receives a structured summary that you can then place back onto the canvas.

Can I use it offline?

Absolutely. The whole app runs in your browser. No server calls except for optional AI synthesis.

What export formats do you support?

JSON (lossless machine‑readable), TXT (human‑readable), Markdown / HTML, and PDF reports. You can also copy an AI‑ready prompt for external tools.

Is there a mobile version?

The web app is fully responsive and touch‑optimised. It works great on iOS and Android without installing anything.

How is my data stored?

Everything stays in your browser’s localStorage. We never see your cards. The Pro and Enterprise plans will offer optional cloud sync.
