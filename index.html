FAQ

Questions, answered straight

Where is my data stored?
Entirely on your device, in your browser's local storage — and only after you accept the storage notice. Nothing is uploaded to us; there is no server, no account, and no analytics. Export JSON backups anytime.
Is Pro really a one-time payment?
Yes. You pay once, get a license key instantly, and unlock unlimited cards on that device. No subscription, no renewal, no locked features appearing later.
What does AI Synthesis cost?
MindCan uses a bring-your-own-key model with DeepSeek. You create a key at platform.deepseek.com and pay DeepSeek directly for usage (typically fractions of a cent per synthesis). Your key is stored only on your device and sent only to DeepSeek.
Does it work on phones and tablets?
Yes — MindCan is built mobile-first: touch drag, two-finger pinch-zoom, swipe-to-dismiss panels, and a dock that adapts to small screens. It works in any modern mobile browser.
Can I move my work between devices?
Yes. Export your canvas as JSON on one device and import it on another — spatial layout, colors, and connections are fully preserved.
What's your refund policy?
Purchases follow the refund policy of the store you buy through (Stripe or Gumroad). If something's broken, contact support and we'll make it right.
